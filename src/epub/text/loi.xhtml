List of Illustrations

  1. A plan of the first floor of Styles. The building is in a horseshoe shape with a courtyard in the middle. Spare rooms surround the courtyard. The rooms of the characters face outwards.

  3. A small piece of half-charred paper with cursive letters “ll” and “and” visible.

  4. A crumpled envelope. Writing is scrawled on it: “possessed,” “I am possessed,” “He is possessed.”
